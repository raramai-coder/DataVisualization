Style Guide

Colours

I chose a light coral pink as the main background color because not only is it beautiful but it's desaturated enough to match with most colors which is important because the home page will contain a lot of movie posters with different colors on them.

White works well as a contrast color to the pink as its bright enough to stand out but also not too jarring.

The highlight color is a deeper hue of pink that stands out from the coral and allows objects to be differentiated from other objects on screen.

I chose the purple for important elements that need to do more than stand out but must actively call the attention of the viewer. Important elements such as the button to go to the data visualisation page since this page is at the core of the purpose of this webste

Fonts

All headings are in Javanesse Text which is highly legible but stylised enough to stand out from the paragraph text and to bring out more character on the site as compared to using a standard font like Arial for example.

Lato bold is used for paragraphs, it is also very legible but stylised enough to suit the aesthetic of the website. Which aims to be stylish in a minimalist but distinguishable way.

Both fonts are dyslexia friendly and highly accessible.

Objects

Elements encapsulate divs, buttons, etc.

All elements, cards and images have rounded corners and a thin border that is a uniform light grey except in special cases.

Cards have their border in the highlight color and have a small drop shadow.
